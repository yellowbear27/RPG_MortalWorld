# Myriad Worlds — 三千大千世界

A xianxia universe and its RPG expression. This repository contains the world canon, lore systems, and the implementation of *The Mortal Game* — an idle RPG built on autonomous AI agents and emergent narrative.

---

## The World: Myriad Worlds

Myriad Worlds (三千大千世界) is a xianxia universe built on a rigorous internal physics and cosmological system rather than generic fantasy conventions.

The setting encompasses 3,000 intersecting planes of existence governed by defined metaphysical laws. The energy field known as Qi is formalised as a rank-2 tensor field — transforming correctly under coordinate changes, obeying conservation laws, and following a 64-hexagram behavioural framework derived from the I Ching. The eight primary hexagrams hold foundational authority over field behaviour. This is the physics of the world, not its decoration.

The world is organised around four civilisational powers, each representing a distinct and corrupted relationship to power, order, and transcendence:

Dong Chen (East) — colonial elite, extractive hierarchy, immortality as institutionalised corruption.
Nan Yan (South) — industrial civilisation, cyclical destruction and renewal, furnace logic.
Xi Yue (West) — military empire, conquest culture, instrumental violence as social order.
Bei Yuan (North) — reclusive mysticism, hidden cosmological knowledge, deliberate withdrawal.

These are not aesthetic factions. They are civilisational archetypes whose internal contradictions drive the world's conflicts.

Central mythic objects include the Hundun Egg (primordial chaos made manifest), the Taotie Axe (apocalyptic weapon embodying consumption without limit), and the Dongfang Coliseum (ritual arena whose operation reveals the true engine of immortality — spectacle powered by structural suffering).

The physics simulation layer for this world is implemented in the companion repository `PhysicsEngine_Xianxia` (Hundun Engine).

## The Novel: The Unluckiest Cultivator

The primary narrative expression of Myriad Worlds is a novel told from the perspective of the structurally excluded — the cursed, the exiled, the discarded.

The protagonist is an autistic exile bearing the Hundun Egg — a burden he did not choose and cannot put down. He gathers a fellowship not of exceptional heroes but of outcasts: a former slave, a ruthless orphan, a beast of burden, and others who survived by existing in the gaps of a world not designed for them.

The story opens in the register of dark comedy and social satire. It ends in tragedy. The tonal arc is deliberate — it mirrors the protagonist's deepening awareness of the world's metaphysical and political horror beneath its ritual surface.

The core thematic argument: ordinary suffering matters more than elite transcendence. The cultivation genre conventionally celebrates extraordinary individuals escaping the common fate. This novel interrogates what the common fate actually costs, and who pays for the transcendence of the exceptional.

## The RPG: The Mortal Game

The Mortal Game is an idle RPG set within the Myriad Worlds universe. Its design philosophy departs significantly from conventional RPG structure.

**Core mechanic:** The player creates a character — defining attributes, background, and starting circumstances. After creation, the player has no further control. The character lives autonomously. Random encounters unfold, each building on previous events within defined probability parameters. The character ages and eventually dies. The player reads the accumulated life story after each 24-hour cycle.

The player's role is that of a creator who cannot intervene — present at origin, absent from destiny.

**Encounter system:** Events are generated by an LLM agent operating within bounded probability distributions. Parameters constrain the impossible — a character's background, social position, and prior events determine the weighted probability space of what can plausibly happen next. The system distinguishes between randomness and emergence: individual events may be probabilistic, but the interaction of multiple autonomous characters produces genuinely unpredictable outcomes no single event could generate.

**World Soul:** An overarching autonomous agent functions as dungeon master. It monitors all character interactions, enforces the physical and cosmological laws of Myriad Worlds, and generates consequences for actions. It does not take sides. It maintains coherence.

**Emergent narrative:** When multiple characters exist simultaneously and begin interacting — forming relationships, competing for resources, influencing each other's trajectories — the system produces narrative outcomes that were not scripted by any single component. This emergence is the game's core technical and experiential feature.

**Format:** Text-based. The richness of the experience is cognitive and narrative, not visual.
