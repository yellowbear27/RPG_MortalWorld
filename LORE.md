# World background for RPG MortalWorld.

## The World

A xianxia world in the middle period. Cultivation sects have existed for centuries.
The hierarchy is settled: immortals at the top, cultivators in the middle, mortals at the bottom.
This is not a story about the top or the middle.

## Mortals

Mortals live ordinary lives in the shadow of extraordinary things.
A sect's territorial dispute can reroute a trade road and destroy a merchant's livelihood.
A cultivator's breakthrough can cause weather events.
A minor immortal passing overhead is a story told in the village for thirty years.

Mortals do not cultivate. They have no qi sensitivity worth developing.
They live, work, age, and die on a human timescale.
Their tragedies are human in scale: debt, illness, flood, bad harvests, family failure.

## Tone

The world is indifferent. Not cruel. Indifferent.
Cultivators are not villains. They simply operate on a different scale and do not notice mortals
the way mortals do not notice insects.

Chronicles of mortal lives are recorded because mortal lives have weight.
Not cosmic weight. Human weight.

## The Chronicle System (V0)

Each chronicle captures one mortal life.
Two events drive the life from start condition to end condition.
The first event is seeded by the player. The second is generated by the engine.

Start states (V0): rich, poor
End states (V0): gout, malnutrition

Both the state system and the event system will expand in later versions.

## Planned Expansions

V1: I Ching hexagram system. Each life begins and ends on a hexagram.
    The hexagram describes the character's condition. Not their fate.
    Disposition attribute (cautious to reckless) shifts probability weights on outcomes.

V2: Interview layer. The player can talk to the character or NPCs at each life stage.

V3: Intergenerational loop. Death produces karma. The exit hexagram passes to the next generation.
    A bloodline accumulates history across lives.

V4+: Multiple bloodlines released into the same world. Civilisation simulation.
     The world state is a compressed ledger of hexagram histories, not a library of full chronicles.
